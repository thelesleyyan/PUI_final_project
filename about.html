About
 

Nice to e-meet you! I’m Lesley Yan.

I am an empathy-driven product designer and a visual storyteller, who is passionate about creating delightful, human-centered experience.

I am currently pursuing a double major in Human-Computer Interaction and Statistics, with a minor in Design at Carnegie Mellon University.

Outside of work, I really enjoy cooking and drawing.
